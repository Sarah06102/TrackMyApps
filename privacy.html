Privacy Policy for TrackMyApps

TrackMyApps is a Chrome extension that lets users save job postings from platforms like LinkedIn directly into a personal dashboard.

What We Collect

We collect your email address and password using Firebase Authentication to identify your account.

Why We Collect It

This is used only to identify your account and store your saved job data securely in Firebase.

What We Don’t Collect

  • No financial data
  • No browsing history
  • No location data
  • No third-party sharing or selling of your data
